Hello! I am a fifth-year Ph.D. Candidate in Technology and Operations Management at INSEAD. My research interests focus on practice-driven problems in sustainable and humanitarian operations. Together with my advisors Luk N. Van Wassenhove and Atalay Atasu, I investigate business model choices to improve resource efficiency in for-profit and humanitarian operations. I believe that using resources efficiently has become increasingly important with climate change, as we recognize the limits of our global ecosystem and the need to manage operations accordingly.

I am on the 2022-2023 academic job market.

I will present at the INFORMS Annual Meeting in Indianapolis on 18 October 2022, 11.00-12.15 AM, Location: M-Marriott 7.
