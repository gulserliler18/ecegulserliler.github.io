I am a fourth-year Ph.D. Candidate in Technology and Operations Management at INSEAD , where I am fortunate to be advised by Luk N. Van Wassenhove and Atalay Atasu . My research interests include sustainable and humanitarian operations. I am specifically interested in operational problems related to circular business models and humanitarian funding.

Education

  • INSEAD Business School , Fontainebleau, France
    • Ph.D. in Management, 2023 (expected) Dissertation co-chairs: Atalay Atasu, Luk N. Van Wassenhove Master in Management Sciences, 2020
  • Eindhoven University of Technology , Eindhoven, Netherlands
    • M.Sc. in Operations Management and Logistics, 2018 Excellence Track in Research, supervised by Jan C. Fransoo
  • Bilkent University , Ankara, Turkey
    • B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering, 2016
